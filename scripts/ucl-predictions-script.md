# UCL Predictions TikTok - Voiceover Script

**Title:** I got Gemini, ChatGPT and Claude to Predict the UEFA Champions League Final
**Duration:** ~60 seconds
**Voice:** Chatterbox Daniel
**Format:** TikTok 9:16

---

## Script (with cue markers)

{{cue:three-ais}} I got three AIs to predict who wins the Champions League...

{{cue:round-sixteen}} Round of sixteen. Eight ties. Three AIs. One trophy.

{{cue:psg-chelsea}} PSG face Chelsea. {{cue:gal-liv}} Galatasaray versus Liverpool. {{cue:rma-mci}} Real Madrid take on Man City. {{cue:ata-bay}} Atalanta versus Bayern. {{cue:new-bar}} Newcastle face Barca. {{cue:atm-tot}} Atletico against Spurs. {{cue:bod-spo}} Bodo Glimt versus Sporting. {{cue:lev-ars}} And Leverkusen face Arsenal.

{{cue:all-agree}} Only ONE thing all three AIs agree on... Arsenal goes through.

{{cue:first-shock}} First shock. ChatGPT picks Galatasaray over Liverpool. Istanbul magic.

{{cue:second-shock}} Then two AIs back Atalanta to knock out Bayern Munich.

{{cue:wildcard}} And here's the wildcard. ChatGPT has Bodo Glimt from Norway... beating Sporting AND Arsenal. A proper fairytale run.

{{cue:quarter-finals}} Quarter-finals. The bracket gets spicy.

{{cue:semi-finals}} Semi-finals. And now it splits.

{{cue:the-final}} The final.

{{cue:chatgpt-pick}} ChatGPT says... Newcastle versus Real Madrid. Newcastle wins.

{{cue:gemini-claude}} But Gemini AND Claude... both say Arsenal.

{{cue:champion}} Two out of three AIs predict Arsenal lift their FIRST EVER Champions League trophy.

{{cue:outro}} Arsenal. Champions of Europe.
