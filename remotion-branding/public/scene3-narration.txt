A good record is only half the job. What happens next is where most systems fall down — but not here. The moment an incident is raised, it moves itself. The site manager investigates — root cause, corrective action. The area manager verifies the action was taken, and signs it off. Then the safety team reviews, and closes it out. And here's the quiet genius: everyone can see the whole story, but each person can only edit their part. The steps advance on their own as the work is done — so you always know exactly where it stands, and whose turn it is. The right person, every step. Nothing falls through the cracks.
